Facility

This refers to facilities with resources and related services used by the scientific community to conduct top-level research in their respective fields, ranging from social sciences to astronomy, genomics to nanotechnologies.

Examples include laboratories, special habitats, libraries, databases, biological archives, etc. A type of facility could be a research infrastructure a higher aggregated entity.
