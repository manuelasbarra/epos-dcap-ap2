+Facility

This refers to facilities with resources and related services used by the scientific community to conduct top-level research in their respective fields, ranging from social sciences to astronomy, genomics to nanotechnologies.

Examples include laboratories, special habitats, libraries, databases, biological archives, etc. A type of facility could be a research infrastructure a higher aggregated entity.

+IriTemplate

An IriTemplate consists of a template literal and a set of mappings.

hydra:IriTemplate

+IriTemplateMapping

This maps a variable used in the template to a property and may optionally specify whether that variable is required or not. The syntax of the template literal is specified by its datatype and defaults to the [RFC6570] URI Template syntax, which can be explicitly indicated by hydra:Rfc6570Template.

hydra:IriTemplateMapping
